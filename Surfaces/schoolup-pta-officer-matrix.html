EP

Emergency PTA · no standing officers

On-demand tier · no elections · attendees draw from General PTA membership when convened

Emergency PTAs don't have standing officers. When convened, the General PTA Chair (or Headmaster, if Chair unavailable) presides; the General PTA Secretary takes minutes. The matrix doesn't track separate Emergency-tier roles because the executive is always the General PTA executive. The Emergency tier exists to track meetings and resolutions, not officers.

Design notes

  • Three person types. Parent officers (most), teacher officers (some — Headmaster ex-officio, Form Masters as Form PTA Secretaries), and external officers (rare — BOG member who sits on PTA). The schema models all three.
  • Multi-hat is the norm, not the exception. Active parents often hold roles at multiple tiers. The matrix is designed to make multi-hat visible (the "+2 other PTA roles" tag on Mrs Asare) without making it confusing.
  • Vacancies are red. Three vacancies in this snapshot · one at General (Organising Secretary), one Gibson House Vice, one Form 1 General Science B Treasurer. PTA Chair sees these on dashboard load.
  • Term ending warnings. Mr Boateng's Financial Secretary role ends June when his son completes Form 3. The system flags 30 days out so the AGM can plan a by-election.
  • Ex-officio roles are read-only. They attach to whoever holds the linked staff role. When the Headmaster changes, the PTA Headmaster ex-officio auto-updates.

Drawer behaviour

  • Search prioritises the relevant pool. For a Form PTA role, parents of that class surface first. For a House PTA role, parents of that House. For General, all school parents.
  • Existing hats are shown. Search results reveal what other PTA roles a person already holds, so admin can decide if loading another hat is appropriate.
  • Conflict callout is informational, not blocking. Multi-hat is allowed by design. The callout exists to make multi-hat visible, not to prevent it.
  • Term end is auto-calculated. From the tier's configured term length (set in PTA structure). Manual override allowed for interim appointments.
  • Election reference is mandatory. Audit needs to know how this person got the role. Free-text but required.
  • SMS notification on assign. Mrs Boateng gets "You've been assigned as Form 1 GS B PTA Treasurer · term 15 May 2026 to 15 May 2028. Reply HELP for more."
