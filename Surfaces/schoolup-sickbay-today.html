How the cluster watch works

  • Six district-aligned categories match what Ghana Health Service tracks for school-age surveillance
  • Thresholds: 4+ cases / 7 days triggers monitor; 8+ or 50% week-over-week rise triggers amber and prompts notification to Wassa Amenfi GHS
  • URTI at 6 cases is in monitor zone · not yet amber, but the matron has informally flagged dorm 4 boys (3 of the 6) for the housemaster
  • Apollo (conjunctivitis) kept on the watch list explicitly · past Ghana-wide outbreaks have spread fast in boarding schools
  • Sports injury tracked here so a sudden spike triggers a campus safety review · separate from infectious surveillance
